Disney World Rankings

The definitive rankings of all rides in Disney World according to Emily

Magic Kingdom was opened on 1st October 1971.

For the purposes of keeping the list fair, I will only include rides I have been on. As Seven Dwarves Mine Train always has a very long wait and Splash Mountain involves getting wet (and has racist undertones) I have not been on either of these and will therefore exclude them from the list.
