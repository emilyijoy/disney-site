Magic Kingdom

Magic Kingdom was opened on 1st October 1971. The central park of the resort, Magic Kingdom is deisgned similarly to Disneyland in California with the hub and spoke design, the hub being Cinderella's castle and the spokes leading to each of the various lands within the park, including Frontierland and Tommorrowland.

  1. Big Thunder Mountain

    2. This here's the wildest ride in the wilderness!
    This is, without a doubt, the best ride in the Magic Kingdom park. It is undoubtedly the most thrilling of the rollercoasters available and offers breathtaking views of the park, especially at night.

  2. Space Mountain

    3. A journey through time and space
    Take a trip into outer space with this classic Disney attraction that is sure to please any park-goer. You might even be lucky and get to ride it with the lights on, a rite of passage for any disney fan.

  3. Pirates of the Caribbean

    4. Yo ho yo ho a pirate's life for me *squark*
    Pirates is the quinticential Disney ride. It is family friendly while still being incredibly fun. It tells a thrilling story and has a great soundtrack you'll be singing as you leave the ride.
