Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom was opened in April of 1999. The park has several areas featuring real animals, including Africa and Asia, as well as a land dedicated to animals of the part (Dinoland USA) and to animals from fantasy (Pandora).

  1. Avatar: Flight of Passage

    2. Combining VR with real movement, this ride takes you on a journey aboard a banshee as you fly around Pandora. It is thrilling and incredibly immersive, solidifying itself as one fo the bvest rides imagineers have made. This exhillerating experience is something that cannot be forgotten easily.

  2. Expedition Everest

    3. All aboard as you journey into the Himalayas! Providing great views of the parks, Expedition Everest takes you up into the mountain to discover what secrets it holds. With an exciting drop and speeds reaching 50mph, this ride is not one to be missed. Legemd has it you might even come into contact with a strange creature up there but that's just a myth... right?

  3. Kilimanjaro Safaris

    4. The quinticential Animal Kingdom ride, this safari offers a glipse at some amazing animals including elephants, giraffes and even Lions. This up close and person experience with such a variety of incredibly creatures is something that is exciting and educational for all ages. While it does not go extremely fast or incorporate amazing technology like other entries in this list, the ability to get so close to the animals while still in a Disney theme park is amazing.
