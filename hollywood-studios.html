  1. Rock n Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith

    2. How about some backstage passes?
    This is, in my opinion, the best ride in the entirety of the Walt Disney World Resort. The ride is a classic but not one that is boring to re-ride, but one that is exciting every single time. The concept of racing to a concert is great and the music? Amazing! The fact that each car plays different songs adds to the re-rideability as you never know which songs you'll get. While it does not have the outdoor aspects that provide and amazing view like the rollercoasters in the other parks, it is the only ride to go upside down. It is also incredibly fast and always provides a thrilling experience.
